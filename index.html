Activism

From 2015, I started to be a volunteer for Mozilla. Since when Firefox was born I used it and Install it in all Computer I used or repaired, and, when I knowed the existence of the FirefoxStudentAmbassador(FSA) Program in Mozilla I wanted to be involved. After 1 year where I partecipate at various campaign promoted by Mozilla, I haved the opportunity to been selected how Regional Ambassador Lead (RAL) for my Nation. I was be the first RAL selected in all the Europe. For me this was be awesome. I the last five months all togheter we are worked hard to create an more impactive program and the results given for me are really fantastic. In only five months we have planned some events and increased the number of active volunteers in the program and in the Italian Mozilla Community. during this experience, I wanted to create an Local community, here in Cagliari, focused to help the people to increase their skills concerning the Web Literacy. With this focus and this goals I created the Mozilla Cagliari Club, a inclusive Club where all people can partecipate to help or learn how use, make and partecipate on the web. Now I've been selected to be a Reps Regional Coach. This role have the goals to helps the various National Communities in the World to identify the best way to increase the partecipation at all their community activities. I hope to update soon this section with some other awesome news, but, the more important thing is that I'm proud to be a part of the fantastic Mozilla family.
